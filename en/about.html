About Dr.Wound

Our Story

It all started in 1998 with a joke about “9 wounds” that changed a physician’s life.

At the time, I was a chief resident treating a 70-year-old woman with multiple diabetic foot wounds—9 of them. I jokingly said, “Granny, you’re so generous, giving me 9 abalones again!” (a pun in Taiwanese).

That joke sparked a lifelong passion for wound care, leading me to pioneer advanced healing strategies across Taiwan.

Our Mission

We aim to merge medical expertise with AI technology to build a human-centered smart wound care platform that delivers personalized, timely, and accurate treatment guidance for every patient.

Founder

Dr. Wen-Chang Cheng is a leading wound care specialist in Taiwan and the creator of the WCC wound classification system.

WCC stands for both "Wound Care Classification" and his initials, symbolizing his mission to establish Wound Care Centers.

Our Vision

Dr.Wound is more than just a product brand—it’s a vision to combine technology, education, and clinical care to help patients avoid amputation and regain quality of life.

AI Applications

This site provides AI-powered wound analysis, product information, learning resources, and clinical case sharing to empower global wound care professionals and patients with better care experiences.
